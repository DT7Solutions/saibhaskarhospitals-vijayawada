Rheumatology Services in Vijayawada – Sai Bhaskar Hospitals

At Sai Bhaskar Hospitals, Vijayawada, we are dedicated to advanced care for arthritis, joint pain, and autoimmune disorders. Our Rheumatology Department ensures accurate diagnosis, holistic treatment plans, and long-term management to help patients regain mobility, reduce pain, and improve their quality of life.

Rheumatology focuses on chronic conditions affecting joints, muscles, bones, and the immune system. Our specialists provide compassionate care supported by modern diagnostics such as antibody testing, imaging, and synovial fluid analysis, combined with evidence-based treatments including DMARDs, biologic therapies, physiotherapy, and lifestyle guidance.

We specialize in treating rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis, lupus, gout, ankylosing spondylitis, psoriatic arthritis, vasculitis, and connective tissue disorders. With a patient-first approach, we aim to prevent complications, preserve mobility, and improve overall well-being.

Our Rheumatology Services Include:

  • Rheumatoid Arthritis, Osteoarthritis & Psoriatic Arthritis
  • Autoimmune Disorders (Lupus, Vasculitis, Scleroderma)
  • Ankylosing Spondylitis & Spinal Inflammation
  • Joint Pain, Stiffness & Swelling Management
  • Gout & Metabolic Bone Diseases
  • Fibromyalgia & Chronic Pain Syndromes
  • Pediatric Rheumatology (Juvenile Arthritis)
  • Soft Tissue & Connective Tissue Disorders
  • Rehabilitation, Physiotherapy & Lifestyle Management

If you are experiencing joint pain, swelling, stiffness, or fatigue, it may be time to consult a specialist. Our rheumatology services, led by Dr. Yogitha Chennupati, provide expert diagnosis and treatment with compassion and care.

Book a consultation today and take the first step towards better joint and immune health with Sai Bhaskar Hospitals, Vijayawada.
