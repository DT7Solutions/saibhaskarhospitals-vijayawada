Service Name

Emergency & Casualty

Available

24/7, 365 Days

Doctor on Duty

Dr. Anil Kumar

Available 24/7, our Emergency & Casualty services are equipped to manage critical medical and surgical situations with precision, swiftness, and compassion. Staffed by experienced trauma surgeons, emergency physicians, and paramedics, the department ensures rapid diagnosis, stabilization, and timely treatment, in a variety of emergency situations in Guntur.

From accident-related injuries, poisoning, or respiratory distress, we handle a wide range of emergencies. Our treatment protocols align with global triage systems, ensuring that the most critical patients receive immediate attention.

The department is backed by round-the-clock access to radiology, a pathology lab, ICU support, and emergency operation theaters. Whether it's a medical event or an orthopedic trauma, our infrastructure is built for swift transitions and effective intervention.

Types of Emergencies Treated

  • Road Traffic Accidents (RTA) & Trauma Injuries
  • Heart Attacks & Cardiac Emergencies
  • Strokes & Neurological Emergencies
  • Fractures, Burns, and Lacerations
  • Poisoning & Overdose Management
  • Respiratory Distress & Asthma Attacks
  • Seizures, Shock, and Syncope
  • Snake Bites, Animal Bites, and Allergic Reactions
  • Obstetric Emergencies

We also specialize in managing complex cases in Guntur, ensuring coordinated emergency care .
