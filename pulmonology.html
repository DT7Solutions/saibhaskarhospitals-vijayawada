Highly Comprehensive Lung Care & Respiratory Treatment in Guntur

For both acute and chronic respiratory conditions, such as asthma, COPD, and pulmonary fibrosis, our pulmonary science division provides outstanding medical treatment. We provide smooth multidisciplinary care with our skilled orthopedic specialists to address Lung infections, Traumatic Chest Injuries.

Being incorporated with modern diagnostic tools and life-saving ventilator assistance, we handle complex interstitial lung diseases.Sai Bhaskar Hospitals is well known for its proficiency in multiple specializations, including pulmonary rehabilitation & sports injury treatment in Guntur.

We operate closely with other specialties for complete patient care & state-of-the-art equipment for accurate and timely diagnosis. Our team of expert pulmonologists help you breathe easy with holistic patient education & preventive strategies.

Our Services Include:

  • Asthma, Allergies & Immunotherapy
  • Smoking Cessation Sessions
  • Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease (COPD)
  • Pneumonia, Bronchitis & Tuberculosis (TB)
  • Managing Ventilator-based Patients
  • Interstitial Lung Disease (ILD)
  • Sleep Apnea & Polysomnography
  • Pulmonary Function Tests (PFTs)
  • Pleural Effusion
  • Lung Cancer (from diagnosis to supportive care)

Our focus is to help you breathe easier and live healthier. We combine patient education, clinical expertise, and modern technology to deliver world-class pulmonology care under one roof.
